For Claire  ·  Your 50th Birthday  ·  à Paris

I hope you've had the most magical day.

You're in Paris, fifty, the city of love —
with people carrying just a portion of the boundless love
this world has for you, and that YOU create wherever you go.

There is nowhere more perfect for you to be.

Thank you for all the years you've been my friend.
For being a rock, a companion on this rollercoaster of life,
an ear — a light in my life.

You brightened up my day and shone bright the first time I met you.
And it is always that way, every single time we get together.

You keep me grounded and sure of myself.
You help me believe in me.

You're the sister I never had.
And, by your grace, Rana — the mother I never had.

You're there when I need you — and I will always be there for you.
You, Jim, your kids, Rana…
everything that means anything to you.

I hope we get to share as many more years as we've enjoyed —
knowing you're just a heartbeat, a text, a call, a drive or flight away.
None of these is too long...
No two points on this globe are far enough away
to weaken how much it means to me to have you in my life.

I love you — and I love that there is someone in this world
who reminds me who I am,
and that I am someone.

Your friend, Rob.
